Dr.Pratik Wanjari

Dr. Pratik Wanjari is an MBBS graduate specializing in general medicine,
known for his expertise in diagnosing and treating a wide range of health conditions.
Beyond his medical practice, he is also a motivational speaker, inspiring others with
his insights on personal growth, wellness, and overcoming challenges. Dr. Wanjari
blends his medical knowledge with a passion for helping individuals lead healthier
and more fulfilling lives, both physically and mentally.
